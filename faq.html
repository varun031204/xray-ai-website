Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is AI-powered X-ray Diagnosis?

AI-powered X-ray diagnosis uses advanced machine learning algorithms to detect diseases like pneumonia, tuberculosis, fractures, and cardiomegaly from X-ray images.

How accurate is the AI diagnosis?

Our AI models have been trained on extensive datasets and achieve high accuracy, but results should always be reviewed by a medical professional.

What diseases can be detected?

The system is capable of detecting pneumonia, tuberculosis, fractures, cardiomegaly, and other lung and bone abnormalities.

How long does it take to get results?

Results are typically generated within a few minutes, allowing for quick and efficient diagnosis.

Is the diagnosis reliable?

While AI diagnosis provides a strong preliminary analysis, a final diagnosis should always be confirmed by a certified radiologist.

How much does the diagnosis cost?

The pricing varies based on the type of scan and analysis. Please visit our pricing page or contact us for detailed fee structures.

Are there any discounts available?

Yes, we offer discounts for senior citizens, students, and bulk medical checkups. Contact us for more details.

How secure is my medical data?

We follow strict data privacy protocols and encryption methods to ensure your medical information remains confidential.
