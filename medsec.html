How Secure is Your Medical Data?

With AI-driven medical diagnostics, data security is a top concern. Patient records, X-ray scans, and reports need robust encryption and compliance with healthcare regulations.

Key Security Measures

  • End-to-End Encryption: Ensures secure data transmission.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can view patient data.
  • Regulatory Compliance: AI systems must follow HIPAA and GDPR guidelines.
